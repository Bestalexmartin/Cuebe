## What Can You Do Right Now?

### Create Your Production
Set up your show with basic information - title, dates, venue, and key people. Think of it as creating the "master folder" for your entire production.

### Build Your Script
Upload existing scripts or start from scratch. Add cues, notes, and timing information that everyone can access and update in real-time.

### Organize Your Team
Set up departments (lighting, sound, costumes, etc.) and assign crew members with the right permissions for their roles.

### Manage Your Venues
Keep track of venue information, technical specifications, and contact details all in one place.
